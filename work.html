WORK ABOUT RESUME

I'm a music-loving, anime-watching, basketball-playing designer passionate about making life easier and more fun for people. Oh, and I might just have a slight obsession with memes too.

I’m currently helping to lead design projects
@ Disrupt and Brainstorm Africa and grow the online
community as a community manager.

Whenever I think about my journey to design, I think it just comes much more from a creative and inventive place. Being interested in sci-fi, iron man - MCU, fantastic four - and more importantly how those films were made and the creativity that went into inventing something that didn’t exist before like Reed Prichard’s smart glasses. It was amazing to see how they interacted with their technology through those interfaces.

Everyday I’m motivated by the opportunity to solve real problems for people. I’m always trying to improve my craft, continue learning, and help others grow as designers.
